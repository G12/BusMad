Enter a Stop and Bus number then tap Bus Status to get the arrival times for the next three buses.

Tap on Map to show bus stops in your vicinity. Tap on a Bus stop icon to get a list of buses servicing this stop.

Tap on Bus Status to get arrival information for buses you select from the list.

When in the Map tap on Stops to add bus stops to the map window.

Double Tap on the map to add stops and zoom in to a specified area.
